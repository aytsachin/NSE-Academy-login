About

NSE Academy Ltd. is a wholly owned subsidiary of the National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. (NSEIL). NSE Academy empowers the next generation of BFSI and FinTech professionals with industry-aligned skills through capacity-building programs and certification courses, powered by an online testing and certification system. The courses are well-researched and carefully crafted with inputs from the industry. NSE Academy works closely with reputed universities and institutions across India in building a competent workforce for the future of BFSI and FinTech. NSE Academy also promotes financial literacy as an essential life skill among youngsters a contribution towards financial inclusion and well-being.
